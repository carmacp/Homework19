360Kids

We redisigned the 360Kids website to simplify the donation process and surface the most sought after information around funding allocation. Our design process started with research, empathizing with our users and understanding their motives, behavioral patterns and pain points. Once we had our data, we started synthesizing our findings to define our persona and problem statement, once we had more clarity, we started brainstorming solutions, then moved into prototyping and lastly testing and reiterating.

Our design Philosphy is to keep things simple yet elegant. We do this by breaking down our clients needs, we look at all possible components and data while respecting our clients parameters. We allow our imagination to take us to new places.
